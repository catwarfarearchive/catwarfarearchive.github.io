What is Cat Warfare?

Cat Warfare is a cat-based, multiplayer, first person arena shooter by Not-So Classic Games originally created in 2015, Being released on Steam in 2017. The game was later remade twice and released as a free-to-play game.

What is this website?

This website is essentially a fansite made by Pikakid98 (Known better in the Cat Warfare community as the announcer) and fully backed by SonicboomColt (The developer) and exists to archive ancient builds of the game for the sake of preservation. Cat Warfare has a long history with many inside jokes between me (Pikakid98) and SonicboomColt such as the music cube, Many classic maps and the blocky cat.

Is this official?

Well, yes. But actually, no. While this website has builds provided by SonicboomColt for the sake of preservation for future players, This website should be considered unofficial as these builds do not represent the final game or it's future updates

Are these builds still playable online?

Despite how old these builds are, they're still playable online!. Meaning that they'll be playable online for the forseeable future
